Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Jujube (developed by Miyo Studio LLC) gives you full control over your account and data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app. This page explains how to do that, what happens when you do, and how to reach us if you need help.

How to delete your Jujube account

Account deletion is available from within the Jujube app. It takes about 30 seconds.

  1. Open the Jujube app and sign in if you're not already.
  2. Tap the Profile icon (top right on the main screen).
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once you confirm, your account and data are removed from our servers immediately. There is no waiting period.

What data gets deleted

When you delete your Jujube account, we permanently remove all of the following:

  • Your profile (email, display name, preferences, taste profile)
  • Your pantry, fridge, and freezer inventory items
  • Your shopping lists and staple items
  • Your saved recipes and favorites
  • Your recipe history and cooking log
  • Your household membership (if you were a sole member, the household and all its data are also deleted; if others are in the household, you are simply removed while the household continues for them)
  • Your authentication account (email/password, Apple, or Google login)
  • All AI usage records associated with your account

What data is retained (and for how long)

After account deletion, we retain nothing personally identifying to you. Specifically:

  • Voice recordings and receipt images: never stored in the first place — they are processed for transcription/OCR then deleted immediately.
  • Anonymized, aggregated statistics (e.g., "N recipes generated last week") may persist in our internal metrics, but they contain no user identifier and cannot be traced back to you.
  • Server logs (used for security and debugging) are retained for up to 30 days and then automatically purged. These may briefly contain IP addresses but no application data.

Partial data deletion

If you want to remove specific data (e.g., delete individual pantry items, remove a recipe from favorites, clear your shopping list) without deleting your entire account, you can do that directly within the Jujube app on the relevant screen. For questions about specific data removal, contact us using the details below.
