Features

What Fridgenie Does

Everything you need to run a smarter, less wasteful kitchen — powered by AI magic.

📦

Smart Pantry

Track everything in your fridge, freezer, and pantry. Get expiry alerts before food goes bad.

🎙

Voice Input

Just say what you bought. Our AI understands natural language and adds items instantly.

🧾

Receipt Scanning

Snap a photo of your receipt. AI extracts items and adds them to your inventory.

👨‍🍳

AI Recipe Discovery

Get personalized recipes based on what you actually have. Choose your cuisine, get match percentages.

🛒

Smart Shopping

Auto-generated shopping lists from your recipes and running-low items. Never forget staples.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Household Sharing

Share your kitchen with family. Everyone stays in sync, no duplicate purchases.
